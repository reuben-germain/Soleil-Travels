Popular Places

Dominica
Perhaps the most beautiful beach on the island, with towering coconut trees and beautiful, gold sand. A great place for a swim, a snorkel or to just soak in the sun.
Mauritius
Mauritius's west coast has unbeatable beaches ranging from the fine white sands of Trou aux Biches in the far north to the isolated Le Morne Peninsulaon the south-west tip of the island.
Seychelles
The Seychelles, considered one of the top honeymoon destinations in the world, is a group of islands in the Indian Ocean known for their beautiful stretches of sand. Located just off the coast of East Africa, this archipelago boasts pristine, white sandy beaches surrounded by turquoise calm waters.
